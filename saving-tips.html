Saving Tips

Learn how to save money wisely with Baon Save. Discover how to save money, create savings goals, and reach your financial objectives more quickly.

Smart Saving Strategies

1. Pay Yourself First

Set aside savings immediately when you receive your allowance. This ensures you save before you spend on non-essentials.

2. The 24-Hour Rule

Wait 24 hours before making non-essential purchases. This helps avoid impulse buying and ensures you really want the item.

3. Use Cash Envelopes

Allocate cash for different spending categories. When the cash is gone, you stop spending in that category for the period.

4. Track Your Progress

Regularly monitor your savings growth. Seeing progress motivates you to continue saving.

5. Automate Your Savings

Set up automatic transfers to your savings account. Out of sight, out of mind - and into savings!

Student-Specific Saving Ideas

  • Bring lunch from home instead of eating out
  • Use student discounts whenever available
  • Share textbooks with classmates
  • Use public transportation or carpool
  • Limit coffee shop visits - make your own
  • Buy second-hand when possible
  • Take advantage of free campus events

Start Small, Think Big

Remember, even small savings add up over time. Saving just ₱50 per day adds up to ₱1,500 per month - that's ₱18,000 per year!
