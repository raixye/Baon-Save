Budgeting

To effectively manage your budget, use Baon Save. To keep you on track, classify your expenditure, track your outlays, and obtain real-time insights.

Why Budgeting Matters

Budgeting is the foundation of financial success. It helps you:

  • Understand where your money goes
  • Identify unnecessary expenses
  • Plan for future purchases
  • Avoid debt and build savings
  • Achieve your financial goals faster

Our Budgeting Approach

At Baon Save, we believe in simple yet effective budgeting strategies:

50/30/20 Rule

Divide your allowance into three categories:

  • 50% for Needs: Essential expenses like food, transportation
  • 30% for Wants: Entertainment, hobbies, dining out
  • 20% for Savings: Building your financial future

Zero-Based Budgeting

Give every peso a purpose. Your income minus expenses should equal zero.
